About This Policy

B-Sync is a smart IoT control application for Brizl and Buenofe devices, including the BUENOFE CO SHIELD carbon monoxide detector. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

The application may collect:

  • Account credentials (phone number, name, OTP verification)
  • Device registration data (device name, room assignment)
  • BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) identifiers for pairing your device
  • Sensor readings from connected devices (CO concentration, temperature, humidity)
  • Push notification tokens for alarm delivery
  • Customer support inquiry content when submitted through the app

How We Use Information

Information is used solely for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Connecting and controlling registered IoT devices
  • Delivering real-time CO, temperature, and humidity alerts
  • Improving app reliability and performance
  • Providing customer support

Data Sharing

B-Sync does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Sensor data and device commands are transmitted to backend servers (AWS, Seoul region) solely for app functionality. Push notifications are delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google). No data is shared with advertising or analytics services.

Data Security

All API communication uses HTTPS. Sensitive credentials are stored using device-level secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). BLE communications use the device manufacturer's proprietary protocol.

Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

Children's Privacy

B-Sync is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
